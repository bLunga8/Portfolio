About Me

My name is Brendan Lunga. I am a graduate student journalist at Nortwestern Medill. I graduated with a bachelor's of arts from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, double majoring in Media and Journalism and History.

Professionally, I have worked as a sports writer at multiple publications, including The Daily Tar Heel, The Daily Northwestern and Keeping It Heel. I have covered, the College World Series, the Olympics and the Super Bowl throughout my career. Sports journalism is my passion, and I plan to continue pursuing it after graduation.
